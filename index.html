Roger's Taxidermy is a small, locally owned and operated shop in Cool Ridge WV. Our mission is to offer great customer service and satisfaction to all of our customers. Mounts are completed in the order we recieve your animal to ensure finished results in a timely fashion. I strive to capture realistic features in every mount completed. Mounts can be customized to include a beautiful habitat or displayed on a traditional plaque.
