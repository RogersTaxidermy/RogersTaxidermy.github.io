Mount'em Up This Fall

Starting this hunting season, September 23, 2019 thru December 31, 2019 Roger's Taxidermy will be giving away a Big Game ladder tree stand along with a Wild Game Nation trail camera. To enter, just bring in your Trophy Whitetail deer into the shop to be mounted. Once your mount is finished, and paid in full, your name will be entered in the drawing to win. The winner will be drawn on January 5, 2020.

Seat Platform: 15.5" x 12"
Ladder: 14" Wide
Foot Platform: 17.25" x 12"
